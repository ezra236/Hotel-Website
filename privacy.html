Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Welcome to Prime Guest Lodge. We are committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have regarding your data.

Information We Collect

When you book a stay or contact us, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment details. This data is provided voluntarily and is used solely to offer you our services.

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us process reservations, enhance our services, and communicate with you about updates, offers, and other information related to Prime Guest Lodge. We take every precaution to ensure that your data is handled securely and responsibly.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties, except when it is necessary to complete your booking (for example, with our payment processors) or when required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data at any time. If you wish to exercise any of these rights or have questions about the information we hold, please feel free to contact us.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at privacy@primeguestlodge.com.
